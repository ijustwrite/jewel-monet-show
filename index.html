Makeup Tips 💄

Real tips from a girl who performs under stadium lights, from school-day soft to game-day bold. Full tutorials live on the show.

💋

Performance lips that LAST

Lip liner all over first, blot, then lipstick. Two quarters and a halftime later? Still perfect.

🧴

Sweat-proof your base

Thin layers beat one thick one. Prime, breathe, set with powder only where you actually shine.

⏰

The 5-minute school face

Brows, mascara, cream blush, gloss. Done. Save the full glam for Friday. Your skin will thank you.

👁️

Lashes under lights

Stadium lights eat subtle. A dramatic lash reads from row 30. That's why performers go big.

😁

Braces? Flex them.

Bold lip colors frame a braces smile beautifully. Cool-toned reds are the secret. Own it. 😁

🌙

Take it OFF right

The real skincare tip: never sleep in performance makeup. Double cleanse, and your future face says thanks.
